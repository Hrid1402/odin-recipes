How to make Pesto Pasta

Anyone can make a Pesto Pasta, but not everyone knows how to make a pesto pasta that’s
slick with plenty of pesto sauce without adding tons of extra oil! Here’s how I make it.

Pesto Pasta Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onion and oil
  • Pesto
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

How to Make Pesto Pasta Step-By-Step

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain.
  2. Cook the onion in oil, then stir in the pesto and seasonings.
  3. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with cheese.
